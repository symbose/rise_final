How To Recycle & Compost

Recycle

Recycling is an important part of conserving natural resources and contributing twords improving the environment!

Many materieals, such as paper, plastic, metal, and glass can be recycled! Check with your cities requirenments for specific instructions.

Compost

Composting is a way to naturally recycle organic materials.

Under proper conditions, organic materials are decomposed by microorganisms, and result in nutrient rich soil.

Check with your city to find a composting program, or build make an at home compost pile if your city doesn't compost.
